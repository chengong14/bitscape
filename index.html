About the Game

A fun and engaging incremental game! Hire workers and strategically drag them to gather resources. Level up your skills to unlock new areas, discover unique resources, and expand your workforce. Inspired by the classic charm of Old School RuneScape.

How to Play

Step 1

Drag workers to work areas

Step 2

Upgrade them to unlock skills
